About PlanU

PlanU was created as a student-focused academic planner designed to help students stay organized, manage assignments, and improve study habits. Many students struggle with balancing deadlines, projects, quizzes, and personal responsibilities throughout the semester. PlanU was designed to provide a simple and organized solution for keeping everything in one place.

Project Goals

The goal of PlanU is to combine assignment tracking, study tools, calendars, and academic resources into one easy-to-use platform. This website demonstrates how technology can help students become more organized and reduce academic stress through planning and time management.

Designed for Students

PlanU is designed for high school and college students who want a better way to organize coursework, prepare for exams, and manage busy schedules. The website focuses on accessibility, organization, and ease of use across different devices and screen sizes.

Future Ideas

Future versions of PlanU could include account logins, assignment uploads, automatic calendar syncing, study reminders, and AI-powered scheduling tools that help students manage assignments more efficiently.
